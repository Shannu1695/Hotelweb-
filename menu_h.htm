Samosa

A popular Indian snack made of crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas.

Pakora

Deep-fried fritters made with a variety of vegetables coated in spiced chickpea flour batter.

Tandoori Chicken

Chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, cooked in a tandoor (clay oven) for a smoky flavor.

Aloo Tikki

Spiced potato patties served with chutneys and toppings like yogurt and chickpeas.

Pani Puri

A popular street food where hollow puris are filled with a spicy tangy mixture of flavored water, tamarind chutney, potatoes, and chickpeas.

Butter Chicken

A rich and creamy chicken curry cooked with tomatoes, butter, and various spices.

Palak Paneer

A vegetarian dish made with fresh spinach and Indian cottage cheese in a spiced gravy.

Rajma Masala

Red kidney beans cooked in a spiced tomato gravy, often served with rice.

Chicken Biryani

A fragrant rice dish cooked with basmati rice, marinated chicken, and aromatic spices.

Punjabi Chole

Spiced chickpea curry often served with deep-fried bread called bhatura.

Gulab Jamun

Soft and spongy milk dumplings soaked in sugar syrup, often served warm.

Kheer

A rice pudding made with milk, rice, and flavored with cardamom and saffron.

Rasgulla

Soft and spongy cottage cheese balls soaked in sugar syrup.

Jalebi

Deep-fried pretzel-like spirals soaked in saffron-flavored sugar syrup.

Kaju Katli

A popular Indian sweet made with cashews and sugar, often served during festivals.

Mango Lassi

A refreshing yogurt-based drink blended with ripe mangoes, sugar, and a hint of cardamom.

Masala Chai

Traditional Indian spiced tea made with black tea, milk, and a mix of aromatic spices.

Thandai

A cold milk-based drink flavored with dry fruits and spices, often served during Holi festival.

Nimbu Pani

Refreshing lemonade made with lemon juice, water, sugar, and a pinch of salt.

Badam Milk

A warm milk drink flavored with almonds and saffron, often enjoyed during winters.
